# Wales Route

Verified against official sources on 2026-07-23. Recheck at answer time.

## Routing Notes

- Use this file only for property in Wales.
- Under the Renting Homes (Wales) framework, renters are commonly “contract-holders” and agreements are “occupation contracts”; mainstream private renting commonly uses a standard occupation contract.
- Do not import English assured-tenancy, Renters' Rights Act, or Right to Rent rules.
- Right to Rent checks do not apply in Wales.

## Checks Before Commitment

- Check the landlord's registration and the responsible landlord/agent's licensing status through Rent Smart Wales.
- Confirm the occupation-contract type and obtain/check the written statement.
- Verify every requested payment against current permitted-payment guidance. Welsh law prohibits several common letting fees and separately regulates holding deposits.
- Confirm the deposit scheme, amount, protection information, and refund/deduction terms.
- Check local HMO licensing when the household and property may qualify.
- Check council tax responsibility and any student disregard/exemption with the local council.
