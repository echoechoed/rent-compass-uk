# Scotland Route

Verified against official sources on 2026-07-23. Recheck at answer time.

## Routing Notes

- Use this file only for property in Scotland.
- A mainstream private tenancy beginning on or after 1 December 2017 is generally a Private Residential Tenancy when statutory conditions are met.
- Do not import English assured-tenancy, Tenant Fees Act, Section 21, or Right to Rent rules.
- Right to Rent checks do not apply in Scotland.

## Checks Before Commitment

- Check the landlord and advertised property against the Scottish Landlord Register or confirm that registration is pending where allowed.
- For three or more unrelated people sharing bathroom or kitchen facilities, check HMO licensing with the local authority.
- Verify the landlord/agent's written terms and the Private Residential Tenancy category.
- Scottish official guidance states that a landlord/agent cannot charge a fee or premium as a condition of granting, renewing, or continuing the tenancy.
- A refundable tenancy deposit must not exceed two months' rent and is generally lodged in an approved scheme within 30 working days of the tenancy starting.
- Check council tax responsibility and student status with the relevant council.
