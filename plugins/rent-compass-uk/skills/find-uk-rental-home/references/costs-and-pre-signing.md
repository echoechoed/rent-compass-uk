# Costs and Pre-signing Checks

## Normalize Cost

Use:

- weekly to monthly: `weekly rent × 52 ÷ 12`;
- monthly to annual: `monthly rent × 12`;
- total recurring cost: rent + council tax/rates + utilities + internet + parking + mandatory recurring charges.

Label estimates and ranges. Do not double-count bills already included.

Separate:

- **Recurring monthly cost**
- **Move-in cash requirement**
- **Potential/conditional cost**

Move-in cash may include a permitted holding payment, tenancy deposit, rent due after signing, moving cost, furniture, parking permit, or operator-specific charge. Verify legality and timing under the applicable nation before describing a payment as due.

## Affordability and Referencing

Do not present an income multiple as a UK-wide legal rule. Ask the advertiser/provider for:

- affordability formula;
- accepted evidence;
- treatment of probation, scholarships, savings, self-employment, overseas income, or benefits;
- guarantor location/income requirements;
- permitted alternatives;
- whether any request for rent in advance is allowed under the applicable current rules.

Never ask the user to upload sensitive evidence to perform a search.

## Before Applying or Reserving

Confirm:

- exact unit, address, rent, availability, term and household;
- who the landlord/agent/operator is;
- which payment, if any, is requested and its refund conditions;
- required applicant information and privacy route;
- viewing or credible remote-viewing evidence;
- unresolved licensing, registration, eligibility, pet, occupancy, or bill questions.

## Before Signing

Check:

- names, address, start date, rent and payment schedule;
- tenancy/contract type under the applicable nation;
- joint or individual liability;
- notice, break, replacement-tenant and early-exit terms;
- deposit amount, protection route and permitted deductions;
- bills, council tax/rates, utilities and mandatory fees;
- repairs, promised works, inventory and furnishing;
- pet, parking, guest, subletting and use restrictions;
- data that differs from the advert or written offer.

Flag terms for qualified legal/housing advice when unclear or high impact.

## Before Paying

Verify:

- payee identity and authority;
- account/invoice details through an independent channel;
- amount, purpose, due date and refund conditions in writing;
- applicable national cap or prohibition;
- deposit-protection information where relevant;
- receipt process.

The skill prepares these checks but does not make the payment.
