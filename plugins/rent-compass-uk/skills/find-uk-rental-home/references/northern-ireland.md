# Northern Ireland Route

Verified against official sources on 2026-07-23. Recheck at answer time.

## Routing Notes

- Use this file only for property in Northern Ireland.
- Do not import English assured-tenancy, Tenant Fees Act, Renters' Rights Act, council-tax, or Right to Rent rules.
- Right to Rent checks do not apply in Northern Ireland.
- Northern Ireland uses domestic rates rather than the council-tax system used in Great Britain; verify the advert/agreement and Land & Property Services position on who pays.

## Checks Before Commitment

- Check that the private landlord is registered through the Northern Ireland Landlord Registration Scheme.
- Check HMO licensing with the local council for qualifying shared accommodation.
- Confirm the written tenancy agreement and the Tenancy Information Notice requirements.
- Current official guidance states that a tenancy deposit cannot exceed one month's rent.
- Current official guidance states that the deposit is generally protected within 28 days and scheme information given within 35 days.
- Verify any agent fee against current Northern Ireland guidance rather than assuming the English fee regime applies.
- Confirm rates, utilities, notice, rent-increase, inventory, and receipt terms.
